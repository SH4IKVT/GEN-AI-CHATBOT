Company Overview

Reliance Industries Limited (RIL) is one of India's largest and most diversified conglomerates with interests in petrochemicals, refining, oil & gas exploration, digital services, and retail. As of 2024, RIL's digital arm Jio Platforms has surpassed 500 million subscribers across India.

Performance Highlights

- Revenue for FY 2023-24: ₹8.3 trillion
- Net profit: ₹72,000 crore
- Digital services revenue growth: 22% YoY
- Retail segment contribution: 28% of total revenue

Sustainability and Future Roadmap

Reliance is actively investing in green energy, including solar and hydrogen technologies. With a ₹75,000 crore allocation over the next 3 years, the company aims to become net carbon zero by 2035.
